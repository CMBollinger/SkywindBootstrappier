What is Skywind

Skywind started as a passion project in 2011 when Skyrim first came out. Fans of the old Morrowind game wanted to port Morrowind over to the Skyrim engine. Although it was eventually decided to do a complete remake of Morrowind in the Skyrim engine. Work on the remake continued slowly for five years until the release of Skyrim: Special Edition with its even newer engine. They then decided to take the leap into remaking it into the Special Edition engine and the march of progress has continued ever since.

What’s Changed in Skywind

Everything! Except for the story and characters of course! The entire game has been rebuilt from scratch and is a complete revamp of the original Morrowind. There are all new 3d models, concept art, voice acting, level design, sound effects and everything else you can think of!tesrskywind.com/gallery/

How to Help

Skywind is made by fans and volunteers all for completely free. They do it solely for the love of the game. If you’re interested in helping please look through the available tasks and send in an application. If you aren’t sure you have the skills to help, they even have courses designed to teach volunteers the skills they need to have in order to help that they call the Arcane University. beyondskyrim.org/au tesrskywind.com/volunteer/

How to play Skywind

As with other total conversion mods in the Elder Scrolls series In order to play Skywind upon its release you will need to own both Morrowind and Skyrim: Special Edition in order for Skywind to work. Once you download the only thing you have left to do is have fun!
